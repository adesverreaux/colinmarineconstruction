Boat Lift Maintenance & Conversions

Swapped out a center console for a pontoon or vice-versa? Have us replace the bunks to lift your new boat safely and securely. At Colin Marine, we have experience making bunk adjustments on all types of lifts. Even if your new boat is similar to its predecessor, small bunk adjustments can be made to more securely harbor your boat. No boat lift is forever, but the life of any lift can be extended with proper maintenance and repairs. At Colin Marine, we replace:

  • Cradle beams
  • Spreader bars
  • Cables
  • Sheeves and pulleys
  • Guideposts
  • Bunks and brackets
  • Motors and covers
We also grease cables and motors, respool cables, and troubleshoot to get lifts running again. We provide a boat lift maintenance plan that includes routine inspections and maintenance on your lift to make sure it's always ready to run when you need it.

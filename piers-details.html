Pier Construction

Piers (perpendicular) or wharfs (parallel) are great additions to any waterfront property. Colin Marine has built new piers up to 550' in length, complete with custom benches, ladders, and various other accessories. Request a quote to learn more about what might be possible at your property.

Pier Repairs & Re-deckings

Colin Marine has the expertise to repair and re-deck any timber framed pier structure. We can replace headers and stringers, complete with the correct hardware, and even relevel piles to the correct elevation. Colin Marine can also extend the life of your pier with pine or composite re-decking!
